## ğŸ’š What Is OpenStreetMap?

OpenStreetMap (OSM) is one of the largest and most important open-source projects in the world.

It is a **global, community-built geographic database**, maintained by millions of contributors who map roads, buildings, paths, land use, and placesâ€”often in regions where no other reliable map exists.

Just as **Wikipedia democratized knowledge**,  
**OpenStreetMap democratized geography**.

Today, OSM powers:
- Navigation and routing
- Emergency and humanitarian response (aid)
- Urban planning and smart cities
- Climate research and environmental protection
- Logistics, mobility, and location intelligence
